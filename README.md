## AR Game
- hold the board with the aruco markers out in front of you so the markers 1 and 3 are at the top
(if you are not sure which markers are 1 and 3, check out this website: https://chev.me/arucogen/ (look at 6x6 and the desired ID))
- make sure all 4 markers are in the camera frame, so they can get recognized. Once they are, the game starts.
- The goal is to destroy as many bricks as you can by bouncing the ball off the paddle at the bottom. You can move the paddle by inserting your finger or a dark pen from the bottom and moving it side to side
